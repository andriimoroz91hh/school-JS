Til Schweiger Biography

Born December 19, 1963 in Freiburg im Breisgau, Baden-Württemberg, Germany Actor, producer, writer, and director Til Schweiger is Germany's best-known actor and also the country's most successful director. With more than 51 Million admissions no other German filmmaker drew more people to cinemas. He runs his own production company Barefoot Films based in Berlin, Germany.
